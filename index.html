    How It Works

    1

    PLACE ORDER ONLINE

    Please select the services required and place order online 24/7. Our services can be ordered individually or as a combined Bundle Package. Prefer to place order over the phone? Please call us FREE at 0800 048 747.

    2

    ASSESSMENT SURVEY BOOKED

    Once you placed order online, our planning team will get get in touch with you to arrange a suitable date and tine to carry out the assessment survey for each service ordered, according to your suitability.

    3

    CERTIFICATE ISSUED

    Certificate / Reports will be issued digitally for each service ordered and sent to you by email, within 48 hours of assessor’s visit. However, if it’s urgent, please advice this in your order notes or let us know when booking appointment(s).
